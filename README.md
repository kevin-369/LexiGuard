LexiGuard | AI-Powered Legal Intelligence & Client Portal
LexiGuard is a high-end legal technology demonstration designed to bridge the gap between complex legal jargon and executive decision-making. Built with a focus on UI/UX accessibility, secure document flows, and AI-assisted workflows, it reflects my experience in digital transformation for the legal and financial sectors.

üöÄ Overview
Drawing from my work with MWC Legal and Protos Capital, LexiGuard addresses the common "bounce rate" and "credibility" issues found in traditional legal portals. It utilizes the Gemini 1.5 Flash API to provide real-time risk assessments and "Plain English" summaries of complex legal documents.

Key Features

Smart Vault: A secure, drag-and-drop document exchange interface built for regulatory compliance.

AI "Plain English" Analysis: Instant translation of legalese into actionable executive summaries.

Dynamic Risk Matrix: Automated identification of high, medium, and low-priority legal risks.


ESG & Governance Tracker: A specialized module for tracking sustainability and corporate governance milestones, inspired by my work with EDGE-certified logistics projects.
